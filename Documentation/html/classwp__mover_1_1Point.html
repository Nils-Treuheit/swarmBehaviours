Defination of a class, Point.

Point is a tuple of coordinates containing latitude, longitude, and altitude in the same order and can be accesed by {lat,lon,alt}, {x,y,z} or {0,1,2}. The altitude of all the aircrafts is fixed to 47 meters to work with only two dimensions(latitude and longitude).
